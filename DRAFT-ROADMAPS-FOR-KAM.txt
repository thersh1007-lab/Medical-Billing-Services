1. ALW BILLING (for existing providers already on the waiver)
-------------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: CONSULT
  - We review your current billing setup and discuss what's working and what's not

Step 2: AUDIT & ONBOARD
  - Review your claims history, identify gaps, and set up our workflow
  - Get access to what we need to start managing your billing

Step 3: ACTIVE MANAGEMENT
  - Submit claims, track statuses, follow up on denials
  - Clear reporting so you always know what's pending, denied, or paid

Step 4: ONGOING OPTIMIZATION
  - Monthly reviews, proactive follow-up on pending/denied claims
  - Revenue tracking and recommendations


2. RCFE LICENSING (new facility or expanding)
---------------------------------------------
Step 1: CONSULT
  - Assess your situation, facility plans, and licensing requirements
  - Determine what's needed for your specific case

Step 2: DOCUMENT PREP
  - Organize all required documents and application materials
  - Checklist specific to your facility

Step 3: SUBMIT
  - Clean application submission to CDSS with proper documentation
  - Follow-through on status and any requests from the state

Step 4: POST-APPROVAL
  - Guidance on next steps after licensing approval
  - ALW enrollment path if applicable


3. ARF LICENSING (Adult Residential Facility)
---------------------------------------------
Step 1: CONSULT
  - Assess your facility plans and ARF-specific requirements
  - Understand the path and sequence

Step 2: DOCUMENT PREP
  - Organized checklist and all required documentation
  - Application materials tailored to your facility

Step 3: SUBMIT
  - Clean application submission with status tracking
  - Follow-through on requests and corrections

Step 4: POST-APPROVAL
  - Next steps after licensing
  - Ongoing compliance guidance


4. ALW WAIVER APPLICATION
-------------------------
Step 1: ELIGIBILITY REVIEW
  - Determine if your facility qualifies for the ALW waiver
  - Identify what's needed to move forward

Step 2: DOCUMENT PREP
  - Organize paperwork, complete required forms
  - Gather all supporting documentation

Step 3: SUBMIT
  - Clean application with proper documentation
  - Nothing missing, nothing incomplete

Step 4: FOLLOW-UP
  - Track application status
  - Respond to state requests and guide you through to approval


5. FUNDING / REVENUE OPTIMIZATION
----------------------------------
Step 1: REVENUE REVIEW
  - Review your current ALW setup and what's limiting results
  - Understand your facility context

Step 2: IDENTIFY LEVERS
  - Pinpoint practical opportunities owners commonly miss
  - Grounded assessment, not overpromising

Step 3: ACTION PLAN
  - Map a clear plan with next actions (not theory)
  - Outline what changes produce the most impact for your situation


6. RCFE CONSULTING
------------------
Step 1: DISCOVERY
  - Understand your facility, goals, and current challenges
  - What are you trying to accomplish?

Step 2: ASSESSMENT
  - Evaluate operations, compliance, and growth opportunities
  - Identify what's working and what needs attention

Step 3: STRATEGIC PLAN
  - Customized consulting deck with actionable recommendations
  - Clear deliverables, not generic advice

Step 4: SUPPORT
  - Ongoing guidance as you execute the plan
  - Follow-up to make sure things are moving
