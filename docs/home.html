Kam’s Corner

KH

Kamil Hershberger

Operator · ALW Billing Specialist · 15+ Years California RCFE/ARF

I’ve been on both sides of the ALW process, as an operator and as the person helping others navigate it. The problems that slow people down are almost always the same: unclear sequencing, documentation that doesn’t match what reviewers expect, and no one to give you a straight answer. That’s exactly what I’ve built this practice to fix.

On billing delays The #1 cause of AR problems isn’t coding errors. It’s missing authorization documentation that was never flagged until claim time.
On licensing timing RCFE licensing and ALW readiness have to be sequenced correctly. Starting ALW too early costs owners 3–6 months of rework.
On follow-through Most providers have a biller. What they don’t have is someone who surfaces issues before they become denials.
