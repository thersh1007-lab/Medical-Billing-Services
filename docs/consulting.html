The consulting phases

This is the structure Cam described: a phased approach. We keep the website simple. The details are covered in the consulting deck and during your consult.

01
Start
Facility plan + licensing path Clarify the facility direction and map the licensing path (what to do first, what to prioritize, and what timing looks like). You'll receive a consulting deck (PowerPoint) and a clear roadmap with priorities.
02
Operate
Operations & readiness Build the systems that make the facility run: roles, routines, documentation readiness, and a repeatable operating rhythm. You'll receive checklists and templates to execute without guessing.
03
Sustain
Sustain and improve Help you maintain consistency over time and improve outcomes as the facility grows (including ALW alignment if relevant). You'll receive a monitoring plan to keep the facility on track.
What happens in Phase 1 (Start)?

We confirm your scenario and timeline, then build a clear sequence plan so you can focus on the right priorities from day one.

What happens in Phase 2 (Operate)?

We focus on practical systems: repeatable routines, team clarity, documentation alignment, and getting the facility confidently ready.

What happens in Phase 3 (Sustain)?

We focus on consistency and improvement over time, with simple monitoring and guidance so the facility stays on track.

What you'll get: a consulting deck (PowerPoint) that explains the roadmap clearly, a short sequence plan (what to do first, next, later), and simple checklists and templates to execute without guessing. If ALW is part of your plan, we'll make sure the consulting roadmap accounts for that.

FAQs

Short answers to common questions.

Is RCFE consulting a one-time call?

It can start as one call, but the full value is usually in phased guidance (roadmap first, then support as you execute).

Do you provide a roadmap?

Yes. The roadmap is the first priority. You’ll also get a consulting deck (PowerPoint) that explains the plan clearly.

Do you help with licensing too?

Yes. Licensing can be part of Phase 1 if you’re starting up or expanding.

Is this legal advice?

No. We provide operational and administrative guidance. For legal advice, consult a qualified professional.
